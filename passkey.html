PassKey Setup

Knovon Helpdesk

Follow these step-by-step instructions to set up PassKey for 2-factor authentication on your Knovon ID. Due to security redirects, you must use the direct settings URL provided below:

Steps to Set Up PassKey

  1. Visit the direct URL for account settings: http://one.knovon.org/enduser/settings.
  2. Log in with your Knovon ID credentials if prompted.
  3. Navigate to the 2-Factor Authentication section and select Setup PassKey.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to add a PassKey. You will be prompted to verify your identity using your current authentication method (e.g., OTP or email verification).
  5. If you are on a supported device, such as an iPhone, Mac, or Windows 10/11 with a biometric scanner, you can choose to use your device's built-in biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, or Windows Hello).
  6. Approve the setup request on your device. Once approved, your PassKey will be securely stored and linked to your Knovon ID account.
  7. After setup, you will see a confirmation message indicating that your PassKey has been successfully configured.

Using PassKey for Authentication

Once PassKey is set up, you can use it to log in securely without needing to enter a password:

  1. Go to https://one.knovon.org.
  2. Select Use PassKey on the login screen.
  3. Approve the login request using your biometric authentication or security key.
  4. You will be logged in securely to your Knovon ID account.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues during setup or login, contact the Knovon Helpdesk for assistance. Ensure your device and browser support PassKey technology, and that your biometric authentication is enabled and functioning correctly.
