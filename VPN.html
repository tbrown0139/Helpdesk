VPN Setup

Knovon Helpdesk

Follow these step-by-step instructions to configure the 1.1.1.1 Zero Trust VPN for your device. Select your platform below:

macOS
iOS
Android

For macOS:

  1. Download and install the Cloudflare WARP Client from this link.
  2. Launch the WARP application and open the settings.
  3. Select "Login with Zero Trust" and enter the domain: knovon.cloudflareaccess.com.
  4. Authenticate with your Knovon ID credentials.
  5. Enable WARP mode and verify the connection is active.

For iOS:

  1. Download the 1.1.1.1: Faster Internet app from the App Store.
  2. Open the app and navigate to account settings.
  3. Select "Login with Zero Trust" and input the domain: knovon.cloudflareaccess.com.
  4. Authenticate with your Knovon ID credentials and enable the VPN.

For Android:

  1. Download the 1.1.1.1: Faster Internet app from the Google Play Store.
  2. Launch the app and go to account settings.
  3. Select "Login with Zero Trust" and enter the domain: knovon.cloudflareaccess.com.
  4. Authenticate with your Knovon ID credentials and toggle the VPN on.
